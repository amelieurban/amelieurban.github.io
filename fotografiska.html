Fotografiska

Challenge: Fotografiska wanted to attract new visitors and boost engagement around a seasonal exhibition.

Concept: I developed an idea for a pop-up activation in central Stockholm, designed to spark curiosity and create a buzz around the museum. The event included interactive photo installations, teaser content for social media, and influencer collaborations.

Result: The concept showed how Fotografiska could reach new audiences, increase social engagement, and drive more footfall to the exhibition.
